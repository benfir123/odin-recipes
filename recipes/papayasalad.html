Papaya Salad

The History of Papaya Salad

Papaya salad originated from the northeast region of Thailand. It consists primarily of vegetables but dried shrimp and crab are also often added. There is another version that adds formented fish. The overall taste of the dish is sour and salty.

Ingredients

  • Papaya
  • Fish sauce
  • Lime
  • Tomato
  • Long beans
  • Peanuts

Steps:

  1. Add fish sauce to morder
  2. Add lime juice
  3. Add chilis
  4. Add Tomatos
  5. Add Dried shrimp
